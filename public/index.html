The first app that adapts text to match a child's reading level

Readable helps kids enjoy reading while they engage with the story, and breaks the cycle of frustration for kids who read below grade level.

By using our app, each child can read the same books as their friends with confidence.

Readable also offers comprehension checks at the end of each chapter to help students improve their reading skills. Questions are auto-graded and displayed on the parent dashboard so they can celebrate their child's progress.
